## 关于联创团队 / About Unique Studio

**联创团队（Unique Studio）是一个由华中科技大学在校学生组成的创新团队。**
**Unique Studio is an innovation-oriented student studio founded and run by students at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.**

团队成立于 2000 年 6 月，秉持“**联众人之志，创非凡之事**”的理念。我们专注于互联网与信息技术，致力于打造一个由精英学生组成的 IT 技术平台。
Founded in June 2000, Unique Studio has upheld the motto “**uniting people’s aspirations to create extraordinary things**.” We focus on the Internet and information technology, and are committed to building an IT platform powered by outstanding students.

团队现位于[启明学院](https://qiming.hust.edu.cn/info/1406/2370.htm)，地址为湖北省武汉市珞喻路 1037 号华中科技大学启明学院亮胜楼 8 楼 808 ~ 811 室。
Unique Studio is currently based at [Qiming College](https://qiming.hust.edu.cn/info/1406/2370.htm), located in Rooms 808–811, 8th Floor, Liangsheng Building, Qiming College, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, 1037 Luoyu Road, Wuhan, Hubei, China.

作为一个以兴趣为导向，强调**主动实践**和**自主创新**的团队，我们推崇**自由、自律、自驱**的团队精神。
As an interest-driven team that values **hands-on practice** and **independent innovation**, we embrace a spirit of **freedom, self-discipline, and self-motivation**.

我们为有潜力、肯努力、有激情的学生提供一流的学习环境，将兴趣转化为核心能力，助你快速成长。在这里，你将体验“老带新”的学习模式，获得来自一线大厂和业界前沿的学长指导，与华中科技大学的 Geek 们共同探讨前沿技术。
We provide a first-class learning environment for students with potential, dedication, and passion, helping them turn their interests into core competencies and grow rapidly. Here, you will experience a mentorship model where senior members guide newcomers, receive advice from alumni working at leading technology companies and industry frontiers, and explore cutting-edge technologies together with the geeks of HUST.
